The Problem

Generic security models fail in mixed environments. A university dorm is not an office park. A museum gallery is not a warehouse. Standard "guards and gates" approaches create friction without safety, leaving specialized institutions vulnerable and their communities alienated.

The Solution

Zwerchau embeds directly into your environment. We live in the space, observe the flow, and build a "pattern library" specific to your unique constraints. We prioritize high-leverage operational changes over expensive hardware overhauls.
